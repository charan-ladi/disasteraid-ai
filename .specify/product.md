# Product Overview

## Name

DisasterAid AI

---

## Objective

Transform disaster information from images, audio, videos, PDFs, and text into structured emergency records.

---

## Target Users

- Disaster Response Teams
- NGOs
- Fire Departments
- District Collectors
- Emergency Volunteers
- Relief Camp Coordinators

---

## Inputs

- Image
- Video
- Audio
- PDF
- Text

---

## Outputs

- Structured JSON
- SQLite Records
- CSV Export

---

## Benefits

- Faster rescue prioritization
- Offline operation
- Centralized emergency information
- Reduced manual work
